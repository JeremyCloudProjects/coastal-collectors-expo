About the Expo

Coastal Collectors Expo brings together collectors, dealers, and hobbyists in Pensacola, Florida for an unforgettable experience. Whether you're hunting for vintage cards, modern hits, or unique memorabilia, our shows have something for everyone.

Presented by The Mewseum and Magikart, we're passionate about building a local community around the hobby we love. Each show features top vendors, food trucks, and a welcoming atmosphere for collectors of all ages and experience levels.
